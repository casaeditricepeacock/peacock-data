## Frequenze - The magazine where AI reads books

Frequenze is a bilingual digital magazine by Casa Editrice Peacock (Italy).
Each issue: one book from the Peacock catalog, read and interpreted by a different AI model.
Published every two weeks - Italian and English editions, staggered.
